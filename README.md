### What they said vs. what they meant 👀

A lighthearted AI-powered tool that translates what people *say* into what they *actually mean* — across every kind of relationship.

Partners, friends, coworkers, family — no one is safe.

<br/>

**"I'm fine."** → *Not fine. You should already know why.*

**"Do what you want."** → *Do what I want.*

**"Sure, have fun."** → *I will remember this.*

## ✨ Features

### 🔮 Relationship Translator
Paste something someone said and get a brutally honest (and hilariously accurate) translation of what they actually meant. Powered by AI, calibrated by every passive-aggressive text you've ever received.

### 🌍 Multilingual
Write in any language — the translator responds in kind. English, Spanish, French, Serbian, you name it. The universal language of *"it's fine"* knows no borders.

### 🎨 Dark & Light Mode
Seamlessly switch between dark and light themes. Your preference is remembered across sessions, and the app respects your system setting out of the box.

### 🖼️ Shareable Cards
Every translation can be turned into a beautifully designed **1080×1080 social card** — complete with speech bubbles, sparkles, and branding. Perfect for Instagram, TikTok, or your group chat.

- **Preview** the card in a modal before sharing
- **Copy to clipboard** as an image with one tap
- **Download** as a PNG
- **Share** via native share sheet or as formatted text

### 📱 Mobile-First Design
Built to feel native on every screen. Sticky translate button on mobile, smooth scroll-to-result, safe area support, and a glassmorphism UI that looks good everywhere.

### ⚡ Compression Ratio
See exactly how much shorter the *real meaning* is compared to what was said. Because the truth doesn't need filler words.

### 🛡️ Rate Limit Handling
Friendly banners with countdown timers if you're translating too fast. No crashes, no cryptic errors — just a gentle "hold on."

## 🎭 How It Works

1. **Paste** what someone said
2. **Tap Translate** 🔮
3. **Read** what they actually meant
4. **Share** the card, the laughs, or the screenshot of the group chat losing it
